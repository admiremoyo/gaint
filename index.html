About

Giant Pillar Enterprises is a trusted Tree Felling, Removals & Truck Hire, and Gardening service provider, established in 2010 in Rustenburg. Initially specialising in tree felling and grass cutting for mining areas, residential homes, farmland clearance, cemeteries, playgrounds, business complexes, and public spaces, the company has grown into a dynamic and leading enterprise.
Today, we proudly operate in Rustenburg and Johannesburg and surrounding areas, while also extending our services to rural and remote communities across South Africa—wherever our clients need us.
At Giant Pillar Enterprises, we pride ourselves on professionalism, flexibility, and customer satisfaction. Our team provides tailored solutions and convenient payment options, ensuring we meet the unique needs of every client. We are committed to creating safe, eco-friendly, and visually appealing environments, while building strong, long-term relationships founded on trust and service excellence.
